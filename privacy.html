Privacy & Data Practices

This page outlines Moventa’s approach to safeguarding your personal information. We are committed to transparency and will publish full policies ahead of public launch.

Data we collect

Moventa gathers the minimum required details to deliver personalized tracking experiences. This typically includes account credentials, movement metrics, optional wellness notes, and diagnostic logs when you request support.

How we use your data

Information fuels adaptive goals, timely reminders, and insights displayed within the app. We never sell personal data and apply industry security practices to protect it.

Your controls

You can request exports or deletion at any time via the in-app support center or by emailing privacy@moventa.app. We will confirm requests within 72 hours.

Policy updates

We will update this notice as features evolve. Material changes will be communicated in-app and via email before they take effect.
